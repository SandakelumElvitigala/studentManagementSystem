Welcome to the Student Registration Portal

The Student Registration Portal is designed to provide a simple and user-friendly way for new students to register for their educational journey. To ensure a smooth and secure registration process, we have implemented several validations and features to protect and enhance your experience.

Applied Validations

  • Date of Birth Validation: The system ensures the date of birth is not a future date.
  • Unique Contact Number Validation: Ensures each student has a unique contact number.
  • Contact Number Format Validation: Only numeric values are accepted in contact number fields.
  • Mandatory Field Validation: Required fields must be completed for successful registration.

How to Use the Portal

  1. Fill in Your Information: Enter your name, contact number, date of birth, and other required details.
  2. Upload Your Profile Image: Click on the image field to upload a profile picture.
  3. Submit: Click the Register button once all fields are completed.
  4. Confirmation: After successful registration, you will receive a confirmation notification.

Security and Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. All personal information provided during registration is securely processed and stored. Strong security measures are in place to ensure your data is protected at all times.
